2004

We began at 265 Changi road in 2004 with a humble shop front, at that time, Hupfarri was known as Hupfarri Hardware. Hupfarri wanted to bring a wide selection of bathroom and kitchenware products to our customers as we realize that Singapore lacked a good local brand. Thus began the search for a quality product we can be proud to call our own.

2006

After scouring all over Asia to find a supplier we can trust and work with, we created our first line of OttO products in 2006. Our first flagship store is located prominently at 261A/C Changi Road.
